Teaching Outline

Our sessions are meant to be taught as a learn as needed compared to standard multiple week online learning courses. The materials are always here, as needed, when it's needed - just simply find the material you need, and make an appointment to learn more!

In the Learning Resources tab, you will find all the completed PDFs and video tutorials we offer, take advantage of it!

Our instructors are available to help guide you through each topic at your own pace, whether you are new to digital forensics or looking to strengthen specific skills. Each session is personalized to focus on your goals and the areas you want to explore, ensuring you get practical, hands-on experience you can apply right away.
