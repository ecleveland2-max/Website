About Us

Our names are Liz Cleveland and Lainey Stanko, and we are excited to offer tutoring sessions and online coursework focused on digital forensics, both the foundational concepts and hands-on experience with industry-standard tools.

Located in downtown Grand Rapids, we created this program to support students in cybersecurity, technology, and related fields who want to deepen their skills or explore digital forensics more intentionally. As a Digital Forensics major whose program was recently discontinued, I am especially passionate about sharing this knowledge and keeping the field accessible for anyone who wants to learn.

Our content will center on step-by-step video tutorials covering tools such as FTK Imager, Autopsy, Wireshark, and other essential forensic applications. We will also provide detailed diagrams, quick-reference PDFs, and practical tips and techniques used throughout the forensic investigation process.

If you are looking to build confidence with forensic software or strengthen your understanding of how digital investigations work, we are here to help you grow.
