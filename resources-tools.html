Tools

These practice tools are designed to help students develop hands-on digital forensics skills. Autopsy provides a step-by-step beginner guide for analyzing drives and recovering data. FTK Imager teaches how to safely create forensic images and preserve evidence integrity. Wireshark helps learners capture and inspect network traffic to investigate suspicious activity. Together, these resources give students practical experience with industry-standard tools.

By working with these tools, students can apply theoretical knowledge in a practical way, exploring real-world scenarios in digital investigations. Each tool is widely used in the industry, so learning them provides both confidence and marketable skills for aspiring forensic analysts.

These resources are flexible, allowing learners to practice at their own pace. Whether reviewing tutorials, following guided exercises, or experimenting with sample data, students can strengthen their understanding of digital forensics techniques while building a strong foundation for more advanced investigations.
